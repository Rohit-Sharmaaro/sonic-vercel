What is Sonic Happy Hour?

If you haven’t heard about Sonic’s Happy Hour, you’re missing out. Typically available every day from 2 PM to 4 PM, this is when Sonic offers some of their most popular items at discounted prices. Whether you’re craving a sweet, refreshing slush or a savory snack, Sonic’s Happy Hour deals have something for everyone.

During Happy Hour, you can score half-price drinks like fountain sodas, slushes, and limeades. Sonic’s classic limeades are a fan favorite—refreshing and tangy, especially when paired with one of their delicious snacks.

Be sure to check out Sonic menu Happy Hour regularly as the deals change, and occasionally, you might find special promotions or new seasonal items.
